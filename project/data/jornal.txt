'tp_despesa' in dataframe indicates the type of expense. Possible types are (Claude):

1) 'Valor Liquidado' (Value Liquidated, The government verifies and confirms 
the supplier delivered the goods or service. The debt is now officially 
recognized, but money hasn't moved yet.)
2) 'Empenhado' (Encumbered, The government reserves the budget amount, 
guaranteeing funds exist for a future expense. Nothing is paid yet — it's 
just a commitment. No spending can happen without an empenho first.)
3) 'Valor Pago' (Value Paid, The actual transfer of money to the creditor. 
The final stage — cash leaves the government and reaches the supplier.)
4) 'Anulação' (Cancellation, Total or partial cancellation of an empenho, 
returning the amount back to the budget when the expense will no longer 
occur.)
